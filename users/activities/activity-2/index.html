On February 29th 2024 was the International Walking Day for Iraq, at 8:30AM students who agreed to attend the trip were being driven to the "Basra Sport City" which is where the Jada El-Nakhla or the Basra International Stadium is situated, multiple groups of students from lots of different schools gathered up and were walking in one straight line until they entered the stadium in which they were welcomed, all of the students in their respective groups walked around the stadium until they have gotten stationed, some stood whilst some stayed on foot, as for the Second Al-Mutafawkeen, we stayed on foot until our time was up, when time was up the Second Al-Mutafawakeen gave their presentation in which was Unique as it was the only one in where the students talk in a microphone including the fact the students were the only one who spoke in English. 3 Students consisting of "Mohammed Nayyef Mohsen", "Ridha Fawzi Tahaa" and "Ammar Wisam", gave their speech in both English and Arabic, all of this process took approximately 2-3 hours (8:30am - ~11:45am).
