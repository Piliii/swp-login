On February 29th 2024 was the International Walking Day for Iraq, at 8:30AM students who agreed to attend the trip were finally being driven to the Sport City Basra which is where the Jada El-Nakhla or the Basra International Stadium, groups of students from lots of different schools gathered up and were walking in one straight line until they entered the stadium itself in which they were welcomed, all of the students in their respective groups walked around the stadium until they have gotten stationed, some stood whilst some stayed on foot, as for the Second Al-Mutafawkeen, they stayed on foot until their time was up, and when time was up they finally gave their presentation in which was Unique as it was the only one where students talk in a mic and also the only one where the students talk in English, 3 students consisting of Mohammed Nayyef Mohsen, Ridha Fawzi Tahaa and Ammar Wisam, the 3 students gave their speech in both English and Arabic and when the speech was finally finished, everyone clapped for the Second Al-Mutafawkeen, all of this process took approximately 2-3 hours, after which the students in their respective groups started going back home.
